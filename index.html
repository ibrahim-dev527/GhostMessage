FAQ

Got questions? Ghosts have answers.

Can the person really not see who messaged them? ▾
100% correct. GhostMessage never stores or reveals sender information. The recipient only sees the message text — nothing else.
Do I need an email address to sign up? ▾
No email required! Just choose a username and password. That's it. We keep signup as frictionless as possible.
How do I share my anonymous message link? ▾
After signing up, your dashboard gives you a unique link. Copy it and share it on WhatsApp, Instagram bio, Twitter, TikTok — anywhere!
Is there a limit to how many messages I can receive? ▾
No limits! Receive as many messages as you want. Spam protection prevents abuse, but genuine messages always get through.
Can I delete messages I don't like? ▾
Absolutely. You have full control over your inbox. Delete any message with one click — it's gone permanently.
Where is my data stored? ▾
Your data lives in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to external servers. Private and local to your device.
