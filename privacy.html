👻 Privacy at a Glance

GhostMessage is built on a simple but powerful promise: senders are always anonymous. Here's our privacy commitment in plain language:

🚫

No Email Required

You never need to provide an email address to use GhostMessage.

🔒

Sender Always Hidden

We never store, log, or reveal who sent a message. Ever.

💾

Local Storage Only

All your data stays in your browser. No central server receives your data.

🚫

No Data Selling

We do not sell, rent, or trade any user data to third parties.

📋 What Data We Collect

GhostMessage collects only the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

Account Information (stored locally in your browser):

  • Username (chosen by you)
  • Display name (optional, chosen by you)
  • Password (encoded and stored locally — never sent to a server)
  • Avatar color preference
  • Account creation timestamp
  • Preference settings (sound, animations, notifications)

Message Data (stored locally):

  • Message text content
  • Message timestamp
  • Read/unread status
✅
What we do NOT collect: Email addresses, phone numbers, IP addresses, device identifiers, location data, browser fingerprints, or any information that could identify a message sender.

💾 How Your Data Is Stored

GhostMessage uses your browser's localStorage API to store all data. This means:

  • All data is stored on your device, not on any remote server
  • Data is not transmitted over the internet to our servers
  • Data is accessible only through the same browser on the same device
  • Clearing your browser data or localStorage will permanently delete your GhostMessage data
  • GhostMessage does not have access to retrieve your data remotely
⚠️
Because data is stored locally, we cannot recover it if you clear your browser. Back up important messages before clearing browser data.

🕵️ Sender Anonymity — Our Core Promise

Sender anonymity is the entire point of GhostMessage. Here's how we guarantee it:

  • No sender tracking: When someone sends you a message, we do not record who they are in any way
  • No IP logging: Sender IP addresses are never logged or stored
  • No session linking: We do not link a sender's browser session to any message
  • No metadata: No metadata that could identify a sender is attached to messages
  • Local processing: Message submission is processed entirely on the sender's device and stored only in the recipient's local browser
🚨
Important: While we guarantee platform-level anonymity, users should not use GhostMessage to send illegal, threatening, or harmful content. Such content may still be subject to investigation by relevant authorities through other means.

🤝 Data Sharing

GhostMessage does not share your personal data with third parties except in the following very limited circumstances:

  • Legal compliance: If required by law, court order, or valid legal process
  • Safety: To protect the safety of users or the public when there is a credible threat of harm

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we technically cannot share it even if we wanted to — we don't have access to it.

We do not use advertising networks, analytics platforms, or any third-party tracking scripts on GhostMessage.

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

GhostMessage does not use cookies for tracking purposes. We use:

  • localStorage: For storing your account and message data locally
  • No advertising cookies
  • No analytics cookies
  • No third-party tracking pixels

Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) is used to load custom typography. This is a standard web request that may be logged by Google as per their privacy policy.

⚖️ Your Data Rights

You have full control over your data on GhostMessage:

  • Right to access: All your data is visible in your dashboard and settings
  • Right to delete messages: Delete individual messages anytime from your dashboard
  • Right to delete account: Delete your entire account from Settings → Danger Zone
  • Right to update: Change your display name, password, and preferences anytime
  • Right to export: Since data is in localStorage, you can access it via your browser's developer tools

Account deletion is immediate and permanent. All associated data is removed with no possibility of recovery.
